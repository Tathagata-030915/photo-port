About Me

Photography isn't just a hobby. It's a way of feeling.

I've always believed that a camera is a license to explore. Based on my journey so far, I don't just look for "pretty" scenes; I look for the story within the frame. Whether it's the raw texture of an old building or the fleeting expression on a stranger's face, I aim to preserve the atmosphere of the moment.

When I'm not behind the lens, I'm observing—learning how light interacts with the world, waiting for that perfect fraction of a second where reality becomes art.

— Through the lens, with passion

My Gear

The tools that help bring my vision to life

Nikon D7500

A versatile DSLR that combines exceptional image quality with intuitive controls. Its 20.9-megapixel sensor and advanced autofocus system make it my trusted companion for capturing life's precious moments.

50mm f/1.8 Prime Lens

The quintessential portrait lens. With its wide f/1.8 aperture, it creates beautiful bokeh and excels in low-light conditions. This lens teaches you to move, to compose, and to see the world from a different perspective.

18-140mm f/3.5-5.6 Kit Lens

Versatility in a single lens. From wide-angle landscapes to telephoto details, this all-in-one zoom lens is perfect for exploring different perspectives without changing equipment. A true workhorse for diverse shooting scenarios.
